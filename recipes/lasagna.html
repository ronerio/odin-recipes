Lasagna Recipe

Description

This classic lasagna recipe features layers of rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and tender pasta sheets, all topped with melted mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Perfectly baked until golden and bubbly, it's a comforting, hearty dish ideal for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • For the meat sauce:
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 onion, finely chopped
    • 2 garlic cloves, minced
    • 500g (1 lb) ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
    • 400g (14 oz) canned crushed tomatoes
    • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • For the béchamel sauce:
    • 3 tablespoons butter
    • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
    • 2½ cups (600 ml) milk
    • A pinch of nutmeg (optional)
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Other layers:
    • 9–12 lasagna noodles (precooked or oven-ready)
    • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
    • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce:
    1. Heat olive oil in a pan.
    2. Sauté the chopped onion until soft, then add the garlic.
    3. Add the ground meat and cook until browned.
    4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and oregano.
    5. Season with salt and pepper. Let simmer for 20–30 minutes until thickened.
  2. Make the béchamel sauce:
    1. In another saucepan, melt the butter.
    2. Add the flour and stir for 1–2 minutes to form a roux.
    3. Gradually pour in the milk, whisking constantly to avoid lumps.
    4. Cook until the sauce thickens.
    5. Season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg (optional).
  3. Assemble the lasagna:
    1. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
    2. Add a layer of lasagna noodles.
    3. Spread a layer of meat sauce, then a layer of béchamel sauce.
    4. Repeat the layers (noodles, meat sauce, béchamel) until all ingredients are used.
    5. Finish with a layer of béchamel on top, then sprinkle mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Bake:
    1. Cover with aluminum foil and bake in a preheated oven at 350°F (180°C) for 25 minutes.
    2. Remove the foil and bake for another 15–20 minutes until golden and bubbling.
  5. Serve:
    1. Let it rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
